Process Consulting

End to end process analysis and redesign to help organizations achieve business strategies in an efficient manner

Streamlining Business Processes

We help clients in redesigning the existing business operations and getting rid of non-value adding costs and activities to achieve significant improvement in productivity, quality, and response time of the business process

Our Approach

Discovery

  • Understanding of existing processes and workflows
  • Analysis and Root Cause Discovery
  • Discussions and brainstorming
  • Finalization of Process Maps and Workflows.

Design

  • Define SOPs, Checklists and guidelines
  • Design As-Is Process Maps
  • Creation of templates for reporting documents i.e. Audit reports, MIS etc

Implementation

  • Establish Quality and Process Improvement Goals
  • Process Training and Internalization
  • Establish Audit and Related Processes to verify compliance and adequacy
  • Establish Measurement and Continual Improvement Process

Tracking

  • Web based Process tracking platform
  • To track all process maps
  • To ensure all the steps are followed in the stipulated timeline
  • All supporting documents are uploaded for paper trail
  • Audit Reports & Certifications
