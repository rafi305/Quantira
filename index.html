    We have got rich experience with our various customers of working on agile methodology. Being iterative in nature and best suited to the current dynamic environments, most of our clients have been adopting this methodology and our teams are experienced and trained enough on this approach. Our cross functional and self-organized technology teams work in collaboration with the teams of our customers and have delivered successfully from multiple locations.

    LeanStartup

    Our teams have also adopted Lean methodology by prioritising the features for MVP (Minimum Viable Product) and delivered those to the customers to launch and then continue improving and adding features. It is preferred not only by start-ups but also by established clients for quicker launch of their products.

    Waterfall & Iterative

    The traditional approach with a flavour of agile by fusing iterations within waterfall is still being followed by some of our clients and worked very well. Our teams are flexible enough to adapt to the changing needs of the clients and deliver. The change management is effectively managed to ensure the product meets the needs of the dynamic environment.

    Staff Augmentation

    Our people work as extended teams of our client’s team from our locations as well as client locations and work together following all the processes of the clients. Our management teams continue monitoring these resources to ensure smooth delivery.
